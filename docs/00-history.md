  - Third service launched was EC2 (August 25)
  - Oct 2009 RDS
  - Dec 2010 Route 53
  - Jan 2012 Dynamo DB
  - july 2015 Code Pipeline
  - Dec 2020 DevOps Guru
- AWS holds 32% of market share as of 2020, Azure 13%, GCP 7% and Alibaba 3%.
